Project Overview

Loved Pets Animal Shelter is a Website dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming pets. The site serves as a central hub for potential adopters to browse available animals, for community members to sign up as volunteers, and for the shelter to share its mission and success stories.

My Role

I led the design and development of the entire website, focusing on:

  • Reviewing Client Brief - Reviewed the Client and what their needs were
  • Reviewing Competitors - Reviewed different competitors of the client and understanding the pros and cons of each.
  • Information Architecture - Structuring content for clarity and ease of navigation
  • Visual Design - Creating a warm, welcoming aesthetic appropriate for an animal shelter
  • Frontend Development - implementing the responsive layout using HTML and CSS
